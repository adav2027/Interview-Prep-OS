### Example (fictional)
- **Case type:** GTM / scaling
- **Prompt:** "We just launched a new POS product to 50 restaurants and want 1,000 by year-end. How do you get there?"
- **Context if asked:** 45-person org, 4 PMs; product has rough edges; support not yet built for scale.
- **Really testing:** segment/sequence logic, choosing the right success metric (gross profit, not signups), and whether you account for operational readiness and a pause trigger.
- **Success looks like:** staggered rollout (top existing accounts first, fold into new-customer onboarding), gross-profit success metric, explicit quality gate tied to core JTBDs.
