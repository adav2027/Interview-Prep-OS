# Acme Cloud — Interview Prep One-Pager (EXAMPLE)

> Fully fictional worked example for candidate **Jordan Rivera**. Shows the end state the OS produces. Acme Cloud and the people below are invented.

**Role:** VP Product · **Date:** TBD · **Format:** Virtual panel (4 interviewers)

## The big picture
Acme Cloud is a ~Series C PLG SaaS company mid-way through a monetization rebuild. The stated competencies (strategy, cross-functional leadership) mask the real question: *can this person own the pricing/packaging turnaround and partner with eng without slowing them down?* Jordan's packaging-overhaul story is the centerpiece of this loop.

## Jordan's anchor stories for this loop
| Competency they'll screen | Best story | One-line outcome |
|---|---|---|
| P&L / monetization | Packaging overhaul turnaround | Conversion 2.1% → 3.4%, NRR 108% → 121% |
| Influence without authority | Influencing a skeptical exec (Sales beta) | Sales went from blocker to partner |
| Ambiguity / leadership | Creating decision rights from chaos | Decision latency: weeks → days |
| Resilience / judgment | The margin-negative experiment | Avoided a bad launch; created a standard guardrail |

## The panel

### Dana Okafor — CEO (30 min)
- **Cares about:** conviction, commercial ownership, why-this-company.
- **Will probe:** "Why leave your Director role now? Why Acme?" and your view on their pricing bet.
- **Best angle:** lead with the packaging turnaround; connect to Acme's rebuild.
- **Ask:** "What has to be true 12 months from now for this hire to be a clear win?"

### Sam Whitfield — VP Engineering (45 min)
- **Cares about:** whether product will make engineers faster or slower.
- **Will probe:** PM/eng operating model; do you spec or co-design?
- **Best angle:** embedded-finance instinct → same with eng; prototype + tight loops.
- **Ask:** "Where does the PM/eng relationship work best and worst here today?"

### Priya Nair — Head of Growth (45 min)
- **Cares about:** experimentation rigor and PLG instincts.
- **Will probe:** how you pick success metrics; a time an experiment misled you.
- **Best angle:** the margin-negative experiment story (right metric vs. vanity metric).
- **Ask:** "How is growth and core product split today — and where does it create friction?"

### Marco Reyes — CFO (30 min)
- **Cares about:** revenue accountability and downside-risk management.
- **Will probe:** how you protect the business while pushing growth.
- **Best angle:** two-sided risk framing; testing on net-new first; gross-profit guardrails.
- **Ask:** "How do you and product currently align on guardrail metrics?"

## Likely tough questions (and Jordan's line)
- "You're a Director — why ready for VP?" → "I already own a $80M line and set strategy across 6 products; what changes at VP is org scope, which my program leadership shows I can do."
- "Why us?" → "You're mid-rebuild on exactly the problem I turned around at Northwind."

## Questions to ask (general)
- "What's the biggest disagreement on the product strategy right now?"
- "If this role is wildly successful in year one, what changed?"

## Logistics & notes
- Virtual; confirm timezone. CEO slot is the fit/conviction read — bring energy and a point of view on Acme's market.
